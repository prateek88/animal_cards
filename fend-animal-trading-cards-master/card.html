Baloo-The Bear

Baloo is a main fictional character featured in Rudyard Kipling's The Jungle Book from 1894 and The Second Jungle Book from 1895.

  • First Appearance: The Jungle Book (1967)
  • Last Appearance: The Jungle Book (2016)
  • Created By: Rudyard Kipling

Baloo, a bear, is the strict teacher of the cubs of the Seeonee wolf pack. His most challenging pupil is the "man-cub" Mowgli. Baloo and Bagheera, a panther, save Mowgli from Shere Khan the tiger and endeavor to teach Mowgli the Law of the Jungle in many of the The Jungle Book stories.
